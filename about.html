Our Journey

How We Got Here

2020

The Idea Sparks

During the global pandemic, our founders realized how broken traditional education was. Estudy was born in a small apartment with a big dream.

2021

First 1,000 Students

We launched our beta with 12 courses and reached our first thousand students in just 3 months — all through word of mouth.

2022

100+ Courses & Seed Funding

We expanded our catalog, brought on world-class instructors, and secured our first round of funding to scale the platform.

2025

12,000+ Learners Worldwide

Today, Estudy serves students in over 80 countries with 500+ courses and a thriving community of passionate learners.
